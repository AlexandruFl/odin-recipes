Some real nice blossoms

Description

I make these every single year because I just simply love them. We make them because theyre just so nice and good. You really should try it.

In the rows below youre gonna get an idea on how to make them and what you need

    Here's what youre gonna need:
  • 1 cup of a nice and smooth peanut butter
  • 1 packet of brown sugar so its nice and tasty
  • 1 packet of white sugar
  • two eggs to help binding the concuction
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • a teaspoon of salt
    Here's how you make them nice and good:
  1. Preheat the oven to 190C
  2. In a large bowl, add them together
  3. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes
